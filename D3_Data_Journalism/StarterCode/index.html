Census Data

Aggregation of Census Data plotted to show average age in each state with percentage of population lacking healthcare.

Correlations Discovered Between Percentage of Population Lacking Healthcare and Age

As expected, there is a negative correlation between age and percentage of the population lacking healthcare. This can be seen as the younger generations struggle to secure jobs that provide healthcare. Another contributing factor could be that healthcare becomes a higher priority the older one gets.
